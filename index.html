Our Story

Sharma Safa Sherwani Store

Sharma Safa Sherwani Store is Jaipur's trusted destination for authentic traditional menswear, nestled in the vibrant lanes of Badi Chaupar near Hawa Mahal Road. Whether you are preparing for a grand wedding, a festive celebration, or simply wish to embrace the regal heritage of Rajasthan, our store offers an exquisite collection of safas, pagris, sherwanis, and accessories — all crafted with superior fabrics and vibrant, timeless designs.

With a reputation built on personalised service, custom tailoring, and a warm no-pressure shopping experience, we take pride in helping every customer find their perfect look. Our skilled artisans can design and tie a safa in as little as 30 minutes, ensuring you leave looking and feeling magnificent. Rated 4.8 stars by our happy customers, we are a one-stop shop for all your traditional wear needs — at prices that respect your budget.
